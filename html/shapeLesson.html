Learn About Shapes

Welcome to the World of Shapes!

Shapes are all around us! From circles to squares, each shape has its own unique properties and characteristics.

Square

A shape with four equal sides and four right angles.

Circle

A round shape with no corners or edges.

Triangle

A shape with three sides and three angles.

Trapezoid

A shape with one pair of parallel sides.

Hexagon

A shape with six sides and six angles.

Octagon

A shape with eight sides and eight angles.

Pentagon

A shape with five sides and five angles.

Rectangle

A shape with four sides and four right angles, where opposite sides are equal.
