Learn About Fruits and Veggies

Welcome to the World of Fruits and Veggies!

Fruits and vegetables are not only delicious but also super healthy! Let's explore some of the most popular ones and learn about their benefits.

Why Are Fruits and Veggies Important?

Fruits and vegetables are packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber that help keep us healthy and strong!

  • Apples are great for your heart.
  • Carrots help improve your eyesight.
  • Bananas give you energy for the day.
  • Broccoli boosts your immune system.

Watch and Learn!

Nurtients

Fruits and vegetables are essential for a healthy diet. They provide the nutrients your body needs to grow, stay strong, and fight off illnesses.

  • Vitamins: Fruits like oranges and strawberries are rich in Vitamin C, which boosts your immune system.
  • Minerals: Vegetables like spinach and broccoli are packed with iron and calcium for strong bones and muscles.
  • Fiber: Apples, carrots, and other produce help with digestion and keep your tummy happy.
  • Energy: Bananas and potatoes provide energy to keep you active all day.
  • Hydration: Fruits like watermelon and cucumber keep you hydrated.
